Good afternoon. My name is Nafis Quaisar, and I am from Jhanjharpur, Madhubani, Bihar.
I am currently pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science from Lakshmi Narain College of Technology, Bhopal.
I completed my senior secondary education from A.N. College, Patna, and my schooling from Don Bosco Convent School, Jhanjharpur.

I am focused on Android development. I have worked with Java, Kotlin, Firebase, Room Database, and MySQL.I use Java to build strong core concepts and practice DSA, and Kotlin as my main language for Android development.
I enjoy building practical applications and learning by working on projects.
So far, I have built two Android applications.

My first project is TuneLyf, a music application that supports both online streaming and offline device music.
The idea came from a problem I personally faced. Most music apps focus either only on online streaming or only on offline songs, which forces users to switch between different apps.
To solve this problem, I built a single app where users can stream music online, play offline songs, create playlists, and listen to music smoothly in the background. This improves the overall user experience.

My second project is Organizer Classes, an ed-tech application for students from class 9 to 12.
I have also taught students from class 9 to 12, and during that time I noticed that Bihar Board English-medium students especially face difficulty in finding proper study resources and previous year questions.
Students often need to search on multiple platforms, which is confusing, time-consuming, and sometimes the required content is not even available.
So, I decided to build one platform where students can easily access notes, important questions, previous year questions, and tests in one place.
The app is useful not only for Bihar Board students but also for CBSE and other state boards.

Additionally, I have completed the NPTEL course in Programming in Java, which helped me strengthen my fundamentals.

Apart from technical skills, I enjoy travelling and listening to music, I believe in continuous learning and keep exploring new technologies.

My short-term goal is to build a strong foundation by learning, working on real problems, and adding value to the team.
My long-term goal is to build strong skills, gain real experience, and in the future create an ed-tech platform.

My strength is problem-solving. I stay calm, break problems into smaller steps, and focus on clear solutions.
For example, while building Organizer Classes, I initially did not have experience in creating a large-scale application. So instead of trying to build everything at once, I broke the app into smaller modules like authentication, content, tests, and notes, and built them one by one. This approach helped me complete the project smoothly and improve my design skills.

My weakness is that sometimes I take extra time trying to make my work perfect. However, while working on projects, I am learning to manage my time better by setting priorities and deadlines.
